How to Make Banana Bread!

Ingredients:

- 3 ripes bananas.

- 2 eggs.

- 2 cups flour.

- 3/4 cups sugar.

- 1 tsp salt.

- 1 tsp baking soda.

- 1/2 cup walnuts.

- 1/3 cup butter

Method:

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F

2. Mash the bananas and butter with a fork

3. Add sugar, flour, eggs, salt, walnuts, and baking soda to mix

4. Pour batter into loaf pan

5. Bake for 60 mins

5. Cool and serve
